# Benevolent-Guardian-Akari

## Description
Benevolent Guardian Akari is a score-based arcade game where Akari must defend her beloved Fumiko from her various enemies and adversaries. She will have to fight her way through many enemy swarms of her fellow classmates. 
 
## Features
- Akari will be able to move around in 360-degree movement across a school walkway with the arrow keys and attack with the "Q" key to punch and the "E" key to kick.
- There will be a swarm of fellow college students trying to harm Fumiko. Each student defeated will earn Akari points.
- Attacking enemies in a consecutive combo will multiply the score the higher it gets.

## Challenges
- Certain students will take more time to defeat than others with stronger attacks and defensive options.
- Bosses will appear to test Akari's abilities.
- Short sentence of any skill or topic that needs to be learnt or researched.

## Outcomes
- Ideal: Akari defeats all students and bosses to free Fumiko and win her full love and eternal companionship
- Minimal: Fumiko defeats all enemies and at least one boss to free Fumiko
